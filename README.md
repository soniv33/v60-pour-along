# V60 Pour Along

A guided brew timer for V60 pour-over coffee. Choose a recipe, set your dose, and follow each pour, wait, and swirl with a live countdown, animated ring, and audio cues — no guesswork, no distractions.

**[Open the app →](https://soniv33.github.io/v60-pour-along)**

## Recipes

| Recipe | Author | Doses | Target time |
|--------|--------|-------|-------------|
| Ultimate One-Cup | James Hoffmann | 1 / 1½ / 2 cups | ~3:00 |
| 4:6 Method | Tetsu Kasuya | 1 / 1½ / 2 cups | 4:00–5:00 |
| April | Patrik Rolf | 1 / 1½ / 2 cups | 3:15–3:45 |
| Classic | Scott Rao | 1 / 1½ / 2 cups | 3:30–4:10 |

All pour weights and step durations scale automatically when you adjust the dose.

## Features

- **4 guided recipes** — each broken into timed pour, wait, and swirl steps
- **Adjustable dose** — quick presets (1 cup, 1½, 2 cups) plus per-gram coffee and water steppers that keep the ratio locked
- **Grind selector** — Fine / Med-Fine / Medium / Coarse saved between sessions
- **Per-step countdown** with an animated ring; drawdown counts up so you finish when the bed is dry
- **Tap to pause / resume** on the timer circle
- **Audio + haptic cues** on each step transition
- **Brew verdict** — post-brew recap with time, grind-adjustment tip, and last-brew history
- **Installable PWA** — add to home screen for a full-screen, offline experience
- **Light and dark mode** — follows your system preference

## Hoffmann One-Cup (15 g / 250 g)

| Time | Step | Target |
|------|------|--------|
| 0:00 | Bloom pour | → 50 g |
| 0:10 | Swirl | Saturate grounds |
| 0:15 | Bloom wait | De-gas until 0:45 |
| 0:45 | Pour 2 | → 100 g |
| 1:00 | Wait | 10 s |
| 1:10 | Pour 3 | → 150 g |
| 1:20 | Wait | 10 s |
| 1:30 | Pour 4 | → 200 g |
| 1:40 | Wait | 10 s |
| 1:50 | Pour 5 | → 250 g |
| 2:00 | Swirl + drawdown | Tap Finish when bed is dry |

Total brew time: **~3:00** — too fast/acidic → finer grind; too slow/bitter → coarser
